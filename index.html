Prof Ngozi Chuma Udeh

"I am committed to using my position as the honourable commisioner to serve the needs and interest of the students and better future of umu akwukwo, ndi anambra"

About

Ngozi Chuma-Udeh is an Educationist and a Professor of English. She is an orator, a novelist, a poet and a dramatist. She holds a double Masters Degrees, (Med) Educational Administration, 1997) and (MA English 2004). She also holds two Doctorate Degrees, PhD Educational Administration (2002), PhD English { Comparative Literature stress } (2010). Ngozi served as the Head, Department of English, Anambra State University for two tenures, Coordinator General English Studies for two tenures, Associate Dean of Arts for two tenures, Dean Faculty of Arts for three academic sessions and Chair, Local Organizing Committee for Faculty of Arts International Conferences for five years. She has supervised postgraduate students (MA, PhD) both in her local University and Internationally. She is an International External Examiner both in Educational Administration and in English Studies.
